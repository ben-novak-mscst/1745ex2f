Directions from Atlanta, GA

  • Take I-75 North to I-575 North
  • Continue on I-575 until you reach Overlook Mountain Road
  • Exit onto Overlook Mountain Road and continue north until you reach Highway 197
  • Exit onto Highway 197 and follow signs to the Redhat town square
  • In Redhat town square, take a right to continue on Highway 197
  • Boon Mountain resort will be on your left, 2.2 miles after the town square
  • Follow the driveway up to the main house. You may park in the front drive while you check in

Directions from Chattanooga, TN

  • Take I-75 South to Exit 333
  • Turn left off of the exit ramp and head east on Highway 52
  • Follow Highway 52 until you reach Highway 197
  • Follow Highway 197 north and follow signs to the Redhat town square
  • In Redhat town square, take a right to continue on Highway 197
  • Boon Mountain resort will be on your left, 2.2 miles after the town square
